Paint Correction Services

Paint correction is the process of removing imperfections from your vehicle's paint surface. These imperfections include swirl marks, scratches, oxidation, water spots, and holograms. Our multi-stage correction process restores your paint to like-new condition, preparing it for ceramic coating.

Why Choose Paint Correction?

Over time, your vehicle's paint accumulates microscopic scratches and swirl marks from washing, environmental contaminants, and daily use. These imperfections diffuse light, making your paint look dull and faded. Paint correction removes these defects, restoring depth, clarity, and reflectivity to your paint.

Enhanced Appearance

Removes swirls, scratches, and oxidation for a mirror-like finish.

Increased Value

Properly corrected and protected paint maintains vehicle resale value.

Better Protection

Creates a perfect surface for ceramic coating adhesion and longevity.

Long-Term Savings

Reduces need for frequent polishing and maintains shine longer.

Ceramic Coating Services

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint, creating a layer of protection. This nano-ceramic technology provides superior protection against UV rays, chemical stains, oxidation, and minor scratches. Unlike traditional waxes, ceramic coatings last for years with proper maintenance.

Benefits of Ceramic Coating

Long-Term Protection

Lasts 2-7 years vs traditional wax (3-6 months).

Enhanced Gloss

Creates a deep, wet-looking shine that lasts.

Easy Maintenance

Dirt, bugs, and contaminants rinse off easily.

Chemical Resistance

Protects against acid rain, bird droppings, and tree sap.

UV Protection

Prevents paint fading and oxidation from sun exposure.

Hydrophobic Properties

Water beads and sheets off, reducing water spots.
